Changing cities, one drone at a time.

 

More than half of the worlds population lives in a city. That's more than 3 billion people, and millions more move to cities every year. With an increase in urban population comes an increase in demand for all the things that make city life possible. New, innovative, and efficient transportation systems need to be devised in order to meet that demand.That's where we come in. Our MAPS platform allows drones to operate in an urban environment in order to fulfill roles in transportation, law enforcement, and more. We designed MAPS to address the current and future needs of cities, and to make it easier for everyone to work, live, and play in cities across the globe.

This is what makes MAPS awesome.

  • Autonomous

    MAPS doesn't connect to outside software or human pilots. Everything a drone needs to use the platform is contained onboard.

  • Efficient

    Pathfinding algorithms provide the shortest and most efficient path between any two points.

  • Economical

    Adoption cost is lower than traditional systems, both in real world dollars as well as opportunity cost.

  • Scalable

    MAPS operates in 3D space, allowing for higher traffic density than traditional transportation systems.
